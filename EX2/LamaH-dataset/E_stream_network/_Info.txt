Description of the attributes:

After loading the shapefiles into a GIS, by default the STRAHLER orders are displayed. Order 1 and 2 are hidden for a better overview.

--------------------------------

1) EU-Hydro_network

The shapefile EU-Hydro_network contains the layers "Canals_I" and "River_Net_I" (see documentation listed below) from the basins Rhine, Elbe and Danube.
A Strahler order of -1 at the EU-Hydro_network indicates artificial channels. 

30 attributes, see documentation at:
https://land.copernicus.eu/user-corner/technical-library/eu-hydro_user_guide.pdf

Reference: 
EEA: EU-Hydro – River Network Database, Version 1.2, European Environment Agency under the framework of the Copernicus programme, available at: https://land.copernicus.eu/imagery-in-situ/eu-hydro/eu-hydro-river-network-database (last access: 22.10.2020), 2019.
